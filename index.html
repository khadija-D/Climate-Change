The Issue: What is Climate Change

Climate change is defined as the long-term shifts in temperature and weather patterns. Since the 1800s, humans have been adding to this drastically with the burning of fossil fuels. Fossil fuels add things called greenhouse into the atmosphere. Greenhouse gases are gases in the atmosphere that trap heat. This causes the overall temperature of the Earth to rise, which can have detrimental consequences.
